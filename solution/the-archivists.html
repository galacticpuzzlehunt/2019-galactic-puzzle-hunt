Author’s Notes

This puzzle started from the question "What do the aliens call Earthly things?". It was fun to brainstorm the different ways in which we could teach the different things through these various artifacts.

The panorama presentation idea came after the second testsolve thought the meta was a little uninteresting. We hope that it made the puzzle more exciting to solve.
